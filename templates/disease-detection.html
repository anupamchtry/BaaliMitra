Late Blight

Fungal Infection

Symptoms

Dark brown spots on leaves with yellowish borders. Leaves may curl and wither. White fungal growth may appear on the underside of leaves in humid conditions.

Scientific Name

Phytophthora infestans

Additional Information

This disease spreads rapidly in cool, wet conditions. Without treatment, it can destroy an entire crop within days.

Treatment Plan for Late Blight

Recommended Treatments

  • Immediate Action

    Remove and destroy all infected plant material to prevent spread.

  • Chemical Treatment

    Apply copper-based fungicide or chlorothalonil to remaining plants.

  • Ongoing Care

    Improve air circulation between plants and avoid overhead watering.
