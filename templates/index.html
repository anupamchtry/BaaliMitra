ABOUT US

Welcome to BaaliMitra!

BaaliMitra, which translates to "farming friend" is an initiative designed to create a nurturing and supportive environment for farmers across Nepal. Our platform leverages technology to provide timely information, expert guidance, and community support.

We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern technological solutions to help farmers increase productivity, reduce costs, and adopt sustainable practices.
